- Migrated onto a completely new infrastructure stack and library
- Added Forge and NeoForge support
- Overhauled item sorting with deeper, metadata-aware ordering so lookalike items (enchantments, armor trims, banner patterns, firework stars, and more) sort in a sensible, stable order
- Added a sort order option: alphabetical or creative-menu order
- Added a "containers first" option to sort shulker boxes, bundles, and other content-holding items ahead of the rest
- Added item grouping to keep variant families together when sorting (all wool colors, all planks, and so on), with its own toggle for each family
- Added support for mods and datapacks to define their own item grouping families
- Added slot locking: hold Ctrl/Cmd and click a slot to keep sort, auto-stack, and transfer-all from touching it, remembered per world
- Added hotbar swapping: hold a key and scroll (or press 1, 2, or 3) to swap a whole main-inventory row into your hotbar on the fly
- Added low-durability alerts with an action-bar warning and anvil ping before a tool or piece of gear wears out
- Added optional auto-replace to swap in a matching item from your inventory right before a tool or piece of equipment breaks
- Added click-and-drag to the button position editors for easier repositioning
- Added independent config toggles so every feature can be turned on or off on its own
